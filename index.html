Human judgment required

2 · Approved context

Policy
30 days from delivery
Exception
Goodwill credit is staff discretion
Requested remedy
Full refund

3 · System decision

Do not invent an exception or promise a refund.

Route to a person.

4 · Human action

Choose whether any goodwill option is appropriate and own the customer-facing decision.

Read the holding draft

Hi Margaret, Your cardigan was delivered on April 24, so the standard 30-day return window has passed. I am sending this to our team to review whether there is any goodwill option available, and we will follow up after they check it. Mia from Aurora Threads

Claim discipline

What the synthetic evaluation can support.

The page reports observable routing behavior only. Client performance requires client data and client review.

Supported by this fixture

  • Which source fields were available to each test case.
  • Whether the test produced a draft candidate, admin-action review, or human escalation.
  • Whether the observed route matched the authored expected route.
  • That shopper-facing sending was disabled for the run.

Requires a client pilot

  • Draft acceptance without a major edit.
  • Edit reasons, routing agreement, and unsafe false negatives.
  • Median review time and unhandled cases.
  • Any claim about SLA, backlog, cost, revenue, staffing, or customer satisfaction.
